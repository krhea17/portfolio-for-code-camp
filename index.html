About Me

Katie Wilson is originally from Texas. She attended the University of North Texas where she received her BA in English Literature and an MA in Technical Communications. She moved to Philly in 2010 when she got a job as a technical writer at Oracle. She received her MBA in Finance in 2014 from Drexel University. After that she worked as the Marketing Director for a small network security company in Philly.

She's a member of the Young Professionals Board for Bethesda Project and completed board training through Young Involved Philadelphia. She has volunteered for many organizations including being Program Manager for the Instructional Design and Learning SIG for the Society for Technical Communication, chairing outreach events for community organizations including Habitat for Humanity and Philabundance. In her free time, she travels and trains for Strongman events at CFCC.
